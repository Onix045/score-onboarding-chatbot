## What is inventory?

Inventory is the stock of products your business has on hand — what you have to sell.

## What is inventory tracking?

Inventory tracking keeps a running count of the products you have on hand. Every time you record a sale, S.C.O.R.E. updates your stock count automatically, so you always know what you have and when it's running low.

## Why should I track inventory?

Knowing your stock levels helps you avoid running out of popular items and avoid over-ordering items that aren't selling. It also saves you from counting everything by hand.

## Low-stock alerts

S.C.O.R.E. can let you know when an item's stock is running low, so you can reorder before you run out.
