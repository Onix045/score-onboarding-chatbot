## What is S.C.O.R.E.?

S.C.O.R.E. (Small Company Operations & Resource Engine) is a simple system that helps small businesses run their day-to-day operations in one place. It brings together four tools — inventory tracking, point-of-sale, basic customer records, and business reporting — so you don't need separate apps or spreadsheets for each one.

## Who is S.C.O.R.E. for?

S.C.O.R.E. is built for small and rural business owners, including people who are new to business software or technology in general. You don't need any technical background to use it.

## What can S.C.O.R.E. do?

S.C.O.R.E. helps you track inventory, record sales at the counter, keep basic customer information, and see simple reports about your business, all in one place.

## What makes S.C.O.R.E. different?

Everything you need is in one place: track what you have, sell it, remember who bought it, and see how your business is doing — without jumping between separate tools.
