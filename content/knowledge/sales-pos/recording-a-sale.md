## What is the point-of-sale (POS) feature?

The point-of-sale feature lets you ring up sales at the counter. You pick the product, confirm the quantity, and complete the sale — similar to using a cash register, but simpler.

## How do I record a sale?

Recording a sale normally takes a few taps: pick a product, confirm the quantity, and complete the sale.

## What happens to stock after a sale?

When a sale is completed, S.C.O.R.E. automatically reduces the inventory count for the products sold, so your stock numbers stay accurate without extra work.
