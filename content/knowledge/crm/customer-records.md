## What is CRM?

CRM stands for customer relationship management. In S.C.O.R.E., this means keeping basic information about your customers — like their name, contact details, and purchase history — so you can recognize repeat customers and serve them better.

## What can I do with customer records?

You can look up a customer's past purchases and keep simple notes, helping you build stronger relationships with the people who shop with you.
