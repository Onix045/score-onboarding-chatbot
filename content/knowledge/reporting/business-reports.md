## What is business reporting?

S.C.O.R.E.'s reporting feature shows you simple summaries of how your business is doing: revenue, your top-selling products, and the health of your stock, all at a glance.

## How do reports help my business?

Reports help you notice trends, like which products sell best, so you can make better decisions about what to stock and when — without digging through spreadsheets.
